# Build Cities That Live and Breathe

Territory Developer is a 2D isometric city builder grounded in real simulation. Every road, river, and ridge shapes how your city grows — there are no cheat codes, only consequences.

## What This Is

A strategy game about the interplay between geography and urban growth. Elevation, water flow, and road networks are first-class mechanics. Zones emerge from the land; the land does not bend to the zones.

- Procedurally generated terrain with elevation, rivers, and shore bands
- Road networks that follow — and reshape — the landscape
- Economy and demand driven by geography, not arbitrary meters
- No scripted scenarios: every city tells a different story
