# About Territory Developer

Territory Developer is a 2D isometric city builder where geography is not decoration — it is the game. Rivers carve valleys, ridges block expansion, and shore bands determine where a port can stand. No terrain is cosmetic; every tile has consequences.

## Vision

Most city builders let you flatten the world. Territory Developer does the opposite: the world flattens your plans. Elevation creates natural districts. River systems impose infrastructure costs. Road networks that ignore the landscape fall apart under load. The goal is a game where mastery comes from reading the land, not overriding it.

## What Makes It Different

- **Isometric terrain simulation** — HeightMap and water flow are first-class mechanics, not background art.
- **Emergent zoning** — Residential, commercial, and industrial demand emerges from geography and road access.
- **No scripted events** — Progression comes from your decisions, not a campaign script.
- **Fully deterministic** — The same seed produces the same city, every time.
